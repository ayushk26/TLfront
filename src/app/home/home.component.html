PROJECTS

Braile Me

When Shyam Shah built his first simple prototype at Tinkerer’s Lab he was perhaps unaware that one day his Braille reading device will become the world’s most affordable, multi-language Digital Braille display. Even today 12 million Indians, the worlds largest blind population, are almost completely dependent on the braille script perforated on hard paper. Yet, in the modern world, most of the resources available are in digital form. Put simply, Braille Me is an input and output device wherein the content can be read and typed. You could insert a USB drive and this Braille device can read the files. You can pair it via Bluetooth to a Smartphone, computer, or tablet. You can also Whatsapp, send emails by typing messages in Braille Me which would then get converted into visual English (or another language) characters. The USP of BrailleMe is its unique magnetic operated actuators technology that enables them to offer the product at a 10x price advantage over competitors.
